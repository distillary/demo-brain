Barnum's foundational principle: keeping expenditure consistently below income through systematic awareness, not penny-pinching. He specifically criticizes false economy—the "penny wise and pound foolish" approach that saves trivially on one item while wasting lavishly on another. True economy is a disciplined [[System]] of maintaining a consistent margin favoring income, requiring ongoing tracking and awareness of spending patterns across categories (necessaries vs. luxuries).
