Barnum asserts that whoever excels all others in their [[Vocation]] and maintains good habits and [[Integrity]] cannot fail to secure abundant patronage and the natural [[Wealth]] that follows. There is always room at the top of any field for the truly excellent. Excellence in one's field, combined with character, guarantees success regardless of market overcrowding. Excellence differs from mere competence by surpassing all others in the same profession.
