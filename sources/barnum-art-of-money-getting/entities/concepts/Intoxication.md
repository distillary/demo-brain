Barnum contends that business requires a clear brain to reason through plans carefully, yet intoxicating drinks muddle the brain and create lassitude, neutralizing the energy essential to success. The "nervine" temporarily makes victims feel rich while actually impairing decisions, resulting in lost opportunities and foolish bargains. Alcohol's effects are particularly dangerous to [[Wealth]]-building because they impair judgment precisely when careful reasoning determines business success.
