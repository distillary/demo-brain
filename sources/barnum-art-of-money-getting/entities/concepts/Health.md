Barnum treats health as the foundational prerequisite for [[Wealth]] accumulation. Without sound health, a person lacks the ambition, incentive, and force needed to make money. He argues that many people in poor health could improve by understanding natural laws. Health enables the work capacity and mental clarity essential for business success.
