The [[Rothschild]]'s maxim "Be cautious and bold" reconciles apparent contradiction. All-caution never dares to succeed; all-boldness is reckless and must fail. Success demands both—careful planning followed by confident action, not mere [[Luck]] that gains are lost immediately.
