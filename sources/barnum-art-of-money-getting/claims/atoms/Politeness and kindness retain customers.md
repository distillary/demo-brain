Politeness and civility are the best [[Capital]] in business. Customers prefer returning to merchants who treat them kindly, while rude treatment guarantees they never return and will direct others elsewhere, destroying business value. A person who insults a paying customer to defend their honor loses both that customer and others they influence to seek different establishments. Business profit depends on customer retention more than personal vindication.
