Barnum illustrates with a gentleman's story: his wife's elegant sofa purchase ($30,000) necessitated matching chairs, sideboards, carpets, and tables; this forced them to build a new house, and the yearly maintenance costs rose to $11,000. One impulsive purchase cascaded into permanent lifestyle expansion.
