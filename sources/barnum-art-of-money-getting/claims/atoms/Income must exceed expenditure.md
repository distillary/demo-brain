[[Economy]] is not penny-pinching but ensuring a consistent margin favoring income over expenditure. Barnum criticizes those who save carelessly in one direction (like a hostess refusing an extra candle for light) while wasting in another (expensive ribbons), likening this to being "penny wise and pound foolish."
