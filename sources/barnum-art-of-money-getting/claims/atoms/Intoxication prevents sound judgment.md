Barnum contends that business requires a clear brain to reason through plans carefully. [[Intoxication]] warps judgment and creates lassitude, neutralizing the energy essential to success. Lost opportunities and foolish bargains result from the "nervine," which temporarily makes victims feel rich while actually impairing decisions.
