Many reach near the goal but lose faith and relax efforts, losing the prize forever. Barnum illustrates with military analogy: two equally educated generals differ only in perseverance; one hesitates ("Got cannon?") and loses, while the persevering one pushes through amid chaos with [[Perseverance]].
