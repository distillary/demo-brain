A young man who begins business with borrowed money is unlikely to succeed because he does not understand the true value of capital through personal effort and sacrifice. Receiving money without earning it leads to losses, as he has no experiential knowledge of what that money represents. [[Wealth]] is best acquired through gradual accumulation of [[Capital]] combined with [[Economy]] and [[Perseverance]]. The process of earning money through effort teaches the value of money and creates the discipline necessary for continued success.
