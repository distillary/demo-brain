A man with a genuinely good article for sale must advertise it persistently in newspapers so the public becomes aware of it. Without advertising creating awareness, even excellent goods bring no return because no one knows about them. A newspaper reader typically requires seven exposures to an [[Advertising]] before purchasing: first seeing without noticing, second seeing without reading, third reading, fourth checking price, fifth discussing with family, sixth readiness to purchase, and seventh actual purchase.
