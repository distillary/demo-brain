[[Integrity]] is more precious than diamonds or rubies because it is the foundation of all financial and personal success. A person with integrity enjoys peace and joy that money cannot purchase. No person can be dishonest without eventually being discovered, and when dishonesty is revealed, nearly every path to success is closed forever. The public properly shuns those suspected of false weights or dishonest dealing. A person known to be strictly honest, even if poor, has access to the community's resources because everyone knows his promises to repay are reliable. Honesty provides access to credit better than [[Wealth]] does.
