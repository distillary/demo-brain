A person who concentrates completely on a single business and hammers at it faithfully will eventually succeed. The focused mind constantly generates valuable improvements that would be missed when attention is divided among many occupations. People who constantly shift from one business to another due to excessive optimism about every new venture remain poor because they lack the focused effort needed for success in any single field.
