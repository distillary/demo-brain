Before strategy or [[Capital]] can matter, the individual must possess the raw capacity to act. [[Physical health and sobriety are prerequisites for wealth creation|Health and sobriety]] supply the energy, judgment, and ambition that sustained work demands. That energy must then be directed wisely: [[Vocational alignment and practical skill determine business viability|choosing a vocation that fits one's aptitude]] and acquiring practical skill prevent the waste of effort in pursuits where success is structurally unlikely. Once aligned, the decisive factor is [[Sustained focused effort and perseverance produce wealth, not luck|concentrated perseverance]], doing one thing with full might rather than scattering attention across many ventures or blaming outcomes on [[Luck]].
