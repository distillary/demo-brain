Before any business strategy can operate, the body and mind must be capable of sustained effort. [[Good health is foundational to success|Good health]] supplies the ambition, incentive, and force that [[Wealth]]-building demands. [[Tobacco undermines health and finances|Tobacco]] erodes this foundation by creating an [[Tobacco]] that drains both vitality and funds, while [[Intoxication prevents sound judgment|intoxication]] directly disables the clear reasoning required for every sound business decision. Barnum treats [[Health]] not as peripheral advice but as the substratum without which all other principles are inert.
