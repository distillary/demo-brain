Barnum treats vocation not as arbitrary but as a matching problem. [[Choose a vocation suited to aptitude|Natural aptitude]] must align with [[Vocation]], or the worker fights uphill permanently. Even the right vocation fails in the wrong setting, because [[Location determines business success|location determines demand]]. The safety net beneath both choices is [[Practical skill prevents destitution|practical skill]], which protects against reversal of [[Wealth]] regardless of circumstances. When aptitude, [[Location]], and skill converge, [[Excellence in one's field cannot fail to succeed|excellence becomes self-rewarding]], there is always room at the top for the person who masters their craft.
