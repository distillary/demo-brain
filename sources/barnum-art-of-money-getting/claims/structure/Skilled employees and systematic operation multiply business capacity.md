Beyond personal oversight, a business must amplify the owner's capacity through tools, people, and process. [[Retain talented employees over time|Experienced employees increase in value daily]] and should not be replaced lightly, especially those who bring [[Brain and judgment matter more than mere labor|intellectual capacity alongside labor]]. Operations themselves benefit from [[System doubles productivity|systematic procedure]], which doubles output and halves effortbut this principle has a limit, because [[Excessive system defeats itself|over-systematization]] creates bureaucratic retrieval loss. The final multiplier is information: [[Stay informed about business developments|reading trustworthy newspapers]] prevents a business from being overtaken by innovations and competitors it never saw coming.
